Crime and Punishment

Author: Fyodor Dostoevsky

Published Year: 1866

Details: Crime and Punishment is a novel written by Fyodor Dostoevsky in 1866. It portrays the main character, Raskolnikov, as a young, arrogant, and indecisive young man who is living in poverty. He decides to kill an elderly pawnbroker and pretends to want to pawn his watch as a way to rehearse the crime.
